GAME RULES

  • In this game, a word will be chosen for you to guess randomly, and you'll begin with 10 vacation items.
  • Click on a letter that you think might be in the word. If your guess is correct, the game will reveal the letter in the blanks. If not, you will lose one vacation item.
  • Continue guessing letters to fill in the word. Each incorrect guess removes another item.

Win or Lose: If you can guess the word before you lose all 10 vacation items, then you win! If all the items are gone before you guess the word, then you lose.

Try again and don't lose your vacation!
